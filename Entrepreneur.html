About Us

Entrepreneurs Association

A student association founded at An-Najah National University works to strengthen students' personalities and develop their entrepreneurial spirit in order to be effective in society. We are working to provide an appropriate environment for the incubation of pilot projects to produce real projects that can be implemented in the form of startups. We receive the registration of members as volunteers in our association to work to spread our message that we carry and are charged with delivering to society in general.
